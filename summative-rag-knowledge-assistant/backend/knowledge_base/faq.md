# Company Knowledge Base

## Password Reset

If a user forgets their password, they can reset it by clicking the **Forgot Password** link on the login page.

A password reset email will be sent to the registered email address. The reset link is valid for 30 minutes.

---

## Travel Reimbursement

Employees can submit travel reimbursement requests through the Finance Portal.

Required documents include:

- Hotel invoices
- Flight tickets
- Taxi receipts
- Meal receipts

Reimbursements are normally processed within 5 to 7 business days after approval.

---

## Leave Policy

Employees receive 20 paid leave days every calendar year.

Unused leave may be carried forward up to a maximum of 5 days.

---

## VPN Access

Employees working remotely must connect using the company VPN.

VPN credentials are issued during onboarding by the IT Support team.

---

## Software Installation

Software requests should be submitted through the IT Service Portal.

Manager approval is required before licensed software is installed.

---

## Laptop Support

Employees experiencing hardware issues should contact the IT Helpdesk.

If required, a replacement laptop will be issued after diagnostics are completed.

---

## Account Unlock

Accounts become locked after five unsuccessful login attempts.

Employees must contact IT Support or wait 30 minutes before attempting to log in again.

---

## Expense Claims

Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of purchase.

Claims submitted after 30 days may require manager approval.

---

## Remote Work

Employees may work remotely up to three days each week with manager approval.

Additional remote work requires HR approval.

---

## Security

Employees must never share passwords or multi-factor authentication codes.

All suspicious emails should be reported to the Security Team immediately.
